The story
behind AlphaGator.

$89B U.S. freight brokerage market — and we're just getting started
Mid-Atlantic Our home market · MD · VA · PA · WV

AlphaGator Logistics was founded on a simple conviction: shippers in the Mid-Atlantic deserve a freight partner who combines the pricing power of a national broker with the personal accountability of someone who genuinely has skin in the game. That's not a marketing line — it's the operating philosophy of a veteran who knows what it means to be responsible for a mission.

The company was built asset-light by design, structured around a lean, specialized operations team that handles every functional layer of the brokerage — carrier sourcing, load coordination, real-time tracking, and billing — with the kind of role clarity you'd expect from a well-run unit. No generalists juggling everything. No dropped loads because someone was spread too thin.

Odenton, Maryland puts us in the heart of the Baltimore-Washington corridor — minutes from BWI, directly accessible via I-97 and Route 32, and a short run from the I-95 Northeast Corridor and the Port of Baltimore. It's a deliberate choice — not a ZIP code picked at random. We're embedded in one of the most freight-dense corridors in the country, and we intend to become one of the most trusted names in it.

We're a growth-stage brokerage, and we own that. It means our clients aren't account numbers in a queue. It means the managing principal knows your lanes. It means when something goes sideways on a load, you're talking to someone who cares — because this company exists because of the people who trust us with their freight.
