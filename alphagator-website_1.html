A seven-step load lifecycle, every time.

Standardization is what turns one good shipment into a thousand good shipments. Every load runs the same playbook.

STEP 01 Load intake You send origin, destination, equipment type, and pickup window. We confirm rate and terms in writing the same business day.
STEP 02 Carrier sourcing Our carrier sales team works the approved network and load boards to find capacity that meets your lane and schedule.
STEP 03 Dispatch Formal rate confirmation, pickup numbers, and all required documentation issued to the carrier and copied to your team.
STEP 04 Track & trace Scheduled check-calls and real-time TMS updates. Exceptions get escalated to you before they become a problem.
STEP 05 Delivery confirmation POD captured at delivery. Any discrepancies documented and resolved before the load is closed in our system.
STEP 06 Invoicing Clean invoices on agreed payment terms — Net 30 standard, customizable for established accounts.
STEP 07 Review & iterate Quarterly business reviews on volume accounts. Lane performance, market rate benchmarking, opportunities for dedicated programs.
