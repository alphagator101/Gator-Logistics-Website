A seven-step load lifecycle, every time.

Standardization is what turns one good shipment into a thousand good shipments. Every load runs the same playbook.

STEP 01
Load intake
You send origin, destination, equipment type, and pickup window. We confirm rate and terms in writing the same business day.
STEP 02
Carrier sourcing
Our carrier sales team works the approved network and load boards to find capacity that meets your lane and schedule.
STEP 03
Dispatch
Formal rate confirmation, pickup numbers, and all required documentation issued to the carrier and copied to your team.
STEP 04
Track & trace
Scheduled check-calls and real-time TMS updates. Exceptions get escalated to you before they become a problem.
STEP 05
Delivery confirmation
POD captured at delivery. Any discrepancies documented and resolved before the load is closed in our system.
STEP 06
Invoicing
Clean invoices on agreed payment terms — Net 30 standard, customizable for established accounts.
STEP 07
Review & iterate
Quarterly business reviews on volume accounts. Lane performance, market rate benchmarking, opportunities for dedicated programs.

Freight brokerage FAQ.

Common questions from shippers evaluating AlphaGator Logistics as their freight broker.

What is a freight broker?

A freight broker is a licensed intermediary that connects shippers who need to move freight with motor carriers who have the capacity to transport it. AlphaGator Logistics negotiates competitive rates, manages load coordination, and ensures compliant on-time delivery — handling the entire logistics process so shippers can focus on their core business.

Is AlphaGator Logistics licensed and bonded?

Yes. AlphaGator Logistics holds active FMCSA Property Broker Authority (MC# 1819235), maintains a $75,000 BMC-84 surety bond as required by federal regulation, and carries contingent cargo liability and general liability insurance.

What freight services does AlphaGator Logistics offer?

AlphaGator Logistics offers full truckload (FTL) brokerage, less-than-truckload (LTL) brokerage, expedited and time-critical freight, and dedicated lane management programs for shippers with recurring freight needs.

What areas does AlphaGator Logistics serve?

AlphaGator Logistics is headquartered in Odenton, Maryland and serves commercial shippers throughout the United States, with a primary focus on the Mid-Atlantic region including Maryland, Virginia, Pennsylvania, West Virginia, and the Washington DC metro area.
